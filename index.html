Visit Kansas City!

Welcome to the City of Fountains and enjoy your visit to the "Paris of the Plains"!

Check out some of our favorite landmarks

Kauffman Center

Take in the orchestra or perhaps the opera in one of the finest performing arts centers in the country.

Nelson Atkins Museum

Take some time to admire world call art collections at the city's crown jewel.

Union Station

"All Aboard!", don't miss your chance to check out a piece of history and other showcases hosted here frequently. It's also the main street car stop in town so make sure to catch a ride!
